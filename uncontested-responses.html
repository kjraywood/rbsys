6.1. Inverted Minors

Single-raise = game-forcing

With 5-cards or more in the major, we raise the minor in preference to bidding the four-card major. In essence, a single raise is a 2/1 in partner’s minor, and the auction proceeds just as after any other 2/1.

We bid our longest suit first with a game force so that:
 • we bid shape more accurately,   enabling more accurate   hand evaluation
 • 1m-1M auctions are clearer
 • establishing a fit and GF   prepares us better for   third-hand intervention
