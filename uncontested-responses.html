5.4. Semi-Forcing 1NT

5.4.1. Opener’s rebid

 ●  2♦ almost always at least four
 ●  3 × ♦ only with   ➤  4-5-3-1   ➤  poor hearts (no 2♥ rebid)   ➤  ∼ 14-16 HCP (no pass or 2♠)
 ●  2♣ usually four,   occasionally three or even two

To reduce the frequency of a short-club rebid:
 ●  open 1NT with (53)(32), 14 HCP,   and decent spot-cards
 ●  rebid 2♥ with 4-5-2-2, decent hearts,   and 14-16 HCP
