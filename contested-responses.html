11. Vs Two Suiters

11.1. Lower for Other

 • Lower of their two suits = other suit
  ➤  next step by opener    = default (nothing more to say)

Thus there are two non-jump bids to show the other suit; a cue-bid of the lower of their two suits, and a natural bid.

 • lower way to show other suit = invitational  • higher way to show other suit = GF

The lower cue-bid is usually below  a natural bid in the other suit, in which case it shows invitatational values and the natural bid, which is higher, is GF.
