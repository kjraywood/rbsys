2.2. Splinters in comp

In competetive auctions where an opponent has shown a suit of 5-cards or longer, then the only splinter is in the opponent’s suit. Jumps to the 4-level in a different suit are fit-showing.

2.3. Penalty Doubles

Partner has made a take-out double, then RHO bids one of the unbid suits.

We have agreed a suit, hence have no further need for take-out tables.

Most same-suit delayed doubles.
