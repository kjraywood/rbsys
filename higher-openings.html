17.5. Interference

  New – Aug 12, 2018

We treat direct interference similarly to when they interfere over our strong notrump opening.

17.5.1. Vs double

 ●  redouble   = values but not ♣-length
 ●  pass = bust or ♣-length

17.5.2. Vs overcall

 ●  double   = values but not length in bid suit
 ●  pass = bust or length in bid suit

17.5.3. Subsequent bidding

Just in interference over our strong NT, opener’s double or redouble is take-out unless responder has shown positive values with a double or penalty pass.
