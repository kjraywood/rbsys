18.6. Interference

  Unusual scheme

Many use double (or redouble) to show a bust, and pass as a generic positive. The lack of shape info leads to awkward auctions.

We do what strong one-clubbers do. Double shows values without length in the bid suit. Pass is either a bust or a trap hand.
